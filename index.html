Introduction


I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Iowa State University, specializing in Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing (NLP). Currently, I am conducting research in NLP focusing on LLM, Information Retrival, Knowledge graph, Entity Linking, Retrieval-Augmented Generation(RAG). Previously, as a Software Engineer in NLP at Technometrics Limited (Dhaka, Bangladesh), I worked on developing state-of-the-art models, including Bengali multi-label topic classification and aspect-based sentiment analysis. My passion lies in AI research, particularly ML,DL,NLP and its applications in language understanding and data science.
